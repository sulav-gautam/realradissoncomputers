Introduction

with an objective of “PROVIDING RIGHT SOLUTIONS…..” we have established “GANDAKI OVERSEAS CONCERN PVT LTD” on the year 1998.
We at GOC firmly believe that quality people are very important assets of any organization that drive the organization towards achievement of its objective.We therefore understand the importance of providing you the most talented and hard working people to work for you.

Welcome Message

Please accept our heartiest and warm welcome to our home page. We believe that this site would be able to provide you with the requisite information about the strength and ability of our company.

GOC started its operations on 17th day of September 1998 with a modest beginning but with the crystal clear motive of being a reliable bridge between the Employer around the Globe and the Employees seeking foreign employment from Himalayan Country of Nepal.

It’s been a very satisfying professional experience with satisfied clientele worldwide.
